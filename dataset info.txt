This dataset contains 8,128 car samples. Each sample contains the following features, which can be used to estimate the selling price of cars.

- name: the car model.
- year: the manufacturing year.
- selling_price: sold price.
- km_driven: total kilometres driven by the car.
- fuel: type of fuel used by the car.
- seller_type: type of seller.
- transmission: transmission type of the car.
- owner: number of previous owners.
- mileage (km/ltr/kg): mileage or fuel efficiency of the car.
- engine: the engine of the car.
